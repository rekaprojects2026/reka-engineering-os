# Sprint 06 Brief — UI/UX Perfect Pass

## Goal
Make the app feel mature, consistent, readable, and truly usable day-to-day.

## Why this sprint exists
The V1/V2 foundation may be functional but visually plain or inconsistent.
This sprint upgrades the app without changing its product scope.

## Required outcomes
- typography polish
- spacing polish
- sidebar polish
- table readability improvement
- form UX improvement
- empty state improvement
- dashboard clarity improvement
- role-based dashboard readability
- button consistency
- section hierarchy consistency

## Important
This is not a redesign from scratch.
This is not a fancy visual makeover.
This is a disciplined perfect pass based on the V2 UI/UX spec.
