# Sprint 04 Brief — Role-Based Access and Role-Based Views

## Goal
Make the app meaningfully different for admin, coordinator, reviewer, and member.

## Why this sprint exists
Different users should not see the same data, same menu, or same dashboard.

## Required outcomes
- role-based navigation
- role-based route protection
- role-based data filtering
- role-based dashboard/content differences
- owner vs coordinator vs reviewer vs member distinction
