# Sprint 03 Brief — Compensation and Payments in IDR

## Goal
Add compensation and payment tracking suitable for freelancers and small internal teams.

## Why this sprint exists
The product needs to support the real freelance-first agency question:
who did what, how much is owed, and has it been paid?

## Required outcomes
- rate master usage
- compensation records
- payment batches
- payment items
- IDR-first workflow
- payment statuses

## Important scope rules
This is not a full accounting module.
This is not enterprise payroll.
This is practical compensation and payment tracking only.
